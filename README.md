# NoForcedPacks

> Disable a server's forced resource pack — or toggle individual textures on and off — from Minecraft's vanilla Resource Packs menu. Client-side Fabric mod, choices persist across restarts, tested on Hypixel.

NoForcedPacks is a **100% client-side Fabric mod** that intercepts the resource packs servers force on you and converts them into optional entries in the vanilla **Resource Packs** menu. Switch the whole forced pack off, or disable just the **individual textures** you don't want — and your choices persist across reconnects and restarts. Nothing is installed on the server. Tested on **Hypixel** and **Hypixel Skyblock**.

## Features
- **Disable server-forced resource packs** — never be stuck with a texture pack you didn't choose.
- **Per-texture control** — keep the textures you like and switch off only the individual ones you don't. Not all-or-nothing.
- **Persistent** — settings survive reconnects and game restarts.
- **Non-destructive** — your own installed and built-in packs are untouched.
- **Client-side only** — works on any server, including ones you don't own.
- **Tested on Hypixel / Hypixel Skyblock.**

## Installation
1. Install [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) and [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api).
2. Download NoForcedPacks from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/noforcedpacks) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/noforcedpacks).
3. Drop the `.jar` into your `mods` folder and launch.

## Usage
Join a server that forces a resource pack, then open **Options → Resource Packs**. The forced pack — and its individual textures — appear as toggles you control. Disable what you don't want; your choice is remembered.

## FAQ
- **How do I disable a server's forced resource pack?** Install NoForcedPacks; it turns the forced pack into a toggle in the vanilla Resource Packs menu.
- **Can I disable only specific textures?** Yes — individual textures, not just the whole pack.
- **Does it work on Hypixel?** Yes, tested on Hypixel and Skyblock.
- **Needed on the server?** No — fully client-side.
- **Do settings persist?** Yes, across reconnects and restarts.
- **Touches my own packs?** No, only server-forced ones.
